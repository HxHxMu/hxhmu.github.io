About HxHxM

HxHxM (Haux Haux Music) is a catalog-driven music project exploring the intersection of ritual, technology, narrative identity, and legacy works. The catalog includes real artists, fictional personas, collaborative constructs, and completed band histories—designed to coexist as a coherent, indexable universe.
